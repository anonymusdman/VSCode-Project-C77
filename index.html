To set up visual studio, you open it and make a new folder on finder. Then you go back to Visual Studio and click on the file icon to the top left. Now, click "open folder" and open the folder you made. Now you can start coding.

I used the console to print my answers on it. To open the console, right click and click on inspect. You should see a Elements screen. Click on console to see my project.
